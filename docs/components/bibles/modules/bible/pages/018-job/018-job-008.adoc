[[v18-8-0]]
=== Job, 8
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v18-8-1]]

^<<v18-8-1,1>>^ Bilʹdad the Shuʹhite then said in reply:

[[v18-8-2]]

^<<v18-8-2,2>>^ “How long will you keep speaking like this?
The words of your mouth are but a mighty wind!

[[v18-8-3]]

^<<v18-8-3,3>>^ Will God pervert justice,
Or will the Almighty pervert righteousness?

[[v18-8-4]]

^<<v18-8-4,4>>^ If your sons sinned against him,
He let them be punished for their revolt;^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “He sent them into the hand of their revolt.”
====

[[v18-8-5]]

^<<v18-8-5,5>>^ But if you would just look to God
And plead with the Almighty for favor,

[[v18-8-6]]

^<<v18-8-6,6>>^ And if you were truly pure and upright,
He would pay attention to you^&#42;1^
And restore you to your rightful place.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “rouse himself for you.”
====

[[v18-8-7]]

^<<v18-8-7,7>>^ And though your beginning was small,
Your future would be great.

[[v18-8-8]]

^<<v18-8-8,8>>^ Ask, please, the former generation,
And pay attention to the things their fathers found out.

[[v18-8-9]]

^<<v18-8-9,9>>^ For we were born only yesterday, and we know nothing,
Because our days on earth are a shadow.

[[v18-8-10]]

^<<v18-8-10,10>>^ Will they not instruct you
And tell you what they know?^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “And bring forth words from their hearts?”
====

[[v18-8-11]]

^<<v18-8-11,11>>^ Will a papyrus plant grow tall where there is no marsh?
Will a reed grow tall without water?

[[v18-8-12]]

^<<v18-8-12,12>>^ While it is still in the bud, not yet plucked off,
It will dry up before any other plant.

[[v18-8-13]]

^<<v18-8-13,13>>^ This is the outcome^&#42;1^ of all who forget God,
For the hope of the godless^&#42;2^ will perish,

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “So are the paths.”
. Or “the apostate.”
====

[[v18-8-14]]

^<<v18-8-14,14>>^ Whose confidence is in vain
And whose trust is as fragile as a spider’s web.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “house.”
====

[[v18-8-15]]

^<<v18-8-15,15>>^ He will lean against his house, but it will not keep standing;
He will try to hold on to it, but it will not last.

[[v18-8-16]]

^<<v18-8-16,16>>^ He is a moist plant in the sunlight,
And his shoots spread out in the garden.

[[v18-8-17]]

^<<v18-8-17,17>>^ In a heap of stones, his roots become entwined;
He looks for a house among the stones.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “He looks at a house of stones.”
====

[[v18-8-18]]

^<<v18-8-18,18>>^ But when he is uprooted^&#42;1^ from his place,
That place will deny him and say, ‘I have never seen you.’

[NOTE]
====
. Or “swallowed up.”
====

[[v18-8-19]]

^<<v18-8-19,19>>^ Yes, that is how he will disappear;^&#42;1^
Then others will spring up from the dust.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “that is the dissolving of his way.”
====

[[v18-8-20]]

^<<v18-8-20,20>>^ Surely God will not reject those who keep integrity;^&#42;1^
Nor will he support^&#42;2^ evil men,

[NOTE]
====
. Or “the blameless.”
. Lit., “take hold of the hand of.”
====

[[v18-8-21]]

^<<v18-8-21,21>>^ For he will yet fill your mouth with laughter
And your lips with shouts of joy.

[[v18-8-22]]

^<<v18-8-22,22>>^ Those hating you will be clothed with shame,
And the tent of wicked ones will be no more.”
