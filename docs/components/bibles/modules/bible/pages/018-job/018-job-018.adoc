[[v18-18-0]]
=== Job, 18
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v18-18-1]]

^<<v18-18-1,1>>^ Bilʹdad the Shuʹhite said in reply:

[[v18-18-2]]

^<<v18-18-2,2>>^ “How long before you stop making such speeches?
Show some understanding so that we may then speak.

[[v18-18-3]]

^<<v18-18-3,3>>^ Why should we be viewed as animals
And be considered stupid^&#42;1^ in your eyes?

[NOTE]
====
. Or possibly, “unclean.”
====

[[v18-18-4]]

^<<v18-18-4,4>>^ Even if you tear yourself^&#42;1^ to pieces in your anger,
Will the earth be abandoned for your sake,
Or will the rock move away from its place?

[NOTE]
====
. Or “your soul.”
====

[[v18-18-5]]

^<<v18-18-5,5>>^ Yes, the light of the wicked will be extinguished,
And the flame of his fire will not shine.

[[v18-18-6]]

^<<v18-18-6,6>>^ The light in his tent will certainly grow dark,
And the lamp over him will be extinguished.

[[v18-18-7]]

^<<v18-18-7,7>>^ His vigorous stride is shortened,
And his own counsel will make him fall.

[[v18-18-8]]

^<<v18-18-8,8>>^ For his feet will lead him into a net,
And he will wander onto its mesh.

[[v18-18-9]]

^<<v18-18-9,9>>^ A trap will seize him by the heel;
A snare will catch him.

[[v18-18-10]]

^<<v18-18-10,10>>^ A rope is hidden for him on the ground,
And a trap lies in his path.

[[v18-18-11]]

^<<v18-18-11,11>>^ Terrors frighten him on all sides
And chase him at his feet.

[[v18-18-12]]

^<<v18-18-12,12>>^ His strength fails him,
And disaster will make him stagger.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “limp.”
====

[[v18-18-13]]

^<<v18-18-13,13>>^ His skin is eaten away;
The most deadly disease^&#42;1^ consumes his limbs.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “The firstborn of death.”
====

[[v18-18-14]]

^<<v18-18-14,14>>^ He is torn away from the security of his tent
And marched to the king of terrors.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “a terrible death.”
====

[[v18-18-15]]

^<<v18-18-15,15>>^ Strangers^&#42;1^ will live in his tent;
Sulfur will be scattered on his home.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “Something not his.”
====

[[v18-18-16]]

^<<v18-18-16,16>>^ His roots will dry up beneath him,
And his branches will wither above him.

[[v18-18-17]]

^<<v18-18-17,17>>^ The memory of him will fade from the earth,
And in the street his name will be unknown.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “he will have no name.”
====

[[v18-18-18]]

^<<v18-18-18,18>>^ He will be driven from the light into the darkness
And chased away from the productive land.

[[v18-18-19]]

^<<v18-18-19,19>>^ He will have no offspring and no descendants among his people,
And he will be without a survivor in the place where he lives.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “the place of his temporary residence.”
====

[[v18-18-20]]

^<<v18-18-20,20>>^ When his day comes, the people in the West will be appalled
And the people in the East will be seized with horror.

[[v18-18-21]]

^<<v18-18-21,21>>^ This is what happens to the tents of a wrongdoer
And to the place of the one who has not known God.”
