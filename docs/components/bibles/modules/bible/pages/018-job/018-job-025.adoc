[[v18-25-0]]
=== Job, 25
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v18-25-1]]

^<<v18-25-1,1>>^ Bilʹdad the Shuʹhite said in reply:

[[v18-25-2]]

^<<v18-25-2,2>>^ “Rulership and fearsome might are his;
He establishes peace in heaven.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “in his heights.”
====

[[v18-25-3]]

^<<v18-25-3,3>>^ Can his troops be numbered?
Upon whom does his light not rise?

[[v18-25-4]]

^<<v18-25-4,4>>^ So how can mortal man be righteous before God,
Or how can one born of a woman be innocent?^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “pure.”
====

[[v18-25-5]]

^<<v18-25-5,5>>^ Even the moon is not bright
And the stars are not pure in his eyes,

[[v18-25-6]]

^<<v18-25-6,6>>^ How much less so mortal man, who is a maggot,
And a son of man, who is a worm!”
