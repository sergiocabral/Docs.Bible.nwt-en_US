[[v19-27-0]]
=== Psalm, 27
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

_Of David._

[[v19-27-1]]

^<<v19-27-1,1>>^ Jehovah is my light and my salvation.
Whom should I fear?
Jehovah is the stronghold of my life.
Whom should I dread?

[[v19-27-2]]

^<<v19-27-2,2>>^ When evil men attacked me to devour my flesh,
My adversaries and my enemies were the ones who stumbled and fell.

[[v19-27-3]]

^<<v19-27-3,3>>^ Though an army should set up camp against me,
My heart will not fear.
Though war should break out against me,
Even then I will remain confident.

[[v19-27-4]]

^<<v19-27-4,4>>^ One thing I have asked from Jehovah
—It is what I will look for—
That I may dwell in the house of Jehovah all the days of my life,
To gaze upon the pleasantness of Jehovah
And to look with appreciation^&#42;1^ upon his temple.^&#42;2^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “to look contemplatively.”
. Or “sanctuary.”
====

[[v19-27-5]]

^<<v19-27-5,5>>^ For he will hide me in his shelter on the day of calamity;
He will conceal me in the secret place of his tent;
High on a rock he will place me.

[[v19-27-6]]

^<<v19-27-6,6>>^ Now my head is high above my enemies who surround me;
I will offer sacrifices at his tent with joyful shouting;
I will sing praises^&#42;1^ to Jehovah.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “make music.”
====

[[v19-27-7]]

^<<v19-27-7,7>>^ Hear me, O Jehovah, when I cry out;
Show me favor and answer me.

[[v19-27-8]]

^<<v19-27-8,8>>^ Speaking for you, my heart has said:
“Seek to find my face.”
Your face, O Jehovah, I will seek to find.

[[v19-27-9]]

^<<v19-27-9,9>>^ Do not hide your face from me.
Do not turn your servant away in your anger.
You are my helper;
Do not forsake me or leave me, my God of salvation.

[[v19-27-10]]

^<<v19-27-10,10>>^ Even if my own father and mother abandon me,
Jehovah himself will take me in.

[[v19-27-11]]

^<<v19-27-11,11>>^ Instruct me in your way, O Jehovah,
Lead me in the path of uprightness because of my foes.

[[v19-27-12]]

^<<v19-27-12,12>>^ Do not hand me over to my adversaries,^&#42;1^
For false witnesses have risen up against me,
And they threaten me with violence.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “to the soul of my adversaries.”
====

[[v19-27-13]]

^<<v19-27-13,13>>^ Where would I be if I did not have faith
That I would see Jehovah’s goodness in the land of the living?^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or possibly, “Surely I do have faith that I will see Jehovah’s goodness in the land of the living.”
====

[[v19-27-14]]

^<<v19-27-14,14>>^ Hope in Jehovah;
Be courageous and strong of heart.
Yes, hope in Jehovah.
