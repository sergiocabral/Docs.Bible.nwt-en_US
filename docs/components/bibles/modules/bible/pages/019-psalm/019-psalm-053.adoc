[[v19-53-0]]
=== Psalm, 53
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

_To the director; in the style of Maʹha·lath.^&#42;1^ Masʹkil.^&#42;2^ Of David._

[[v19-53-1]]

^<<v19-53-1,1>>^ The foolish^&#42;1^ one says in his heart:
“There is no Jehovah.”
Their unrighteous actions are corrupt and detestable;
No one is doing good.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “senseless.”
====

[[v19-53-2]]

^<<v19-53-2,2>>^ But God looks down from heaven on the sons of men
To see whether anyone has insight, whether anyone is seeking Jehovah.

[[v19-53-3]]

^<<v19-53-3,3>>^ They have all turned away;
They are all alike corrupt.
No one is doing good,
Not even one.

[[v19-53-4]]

^<<v19-53-4,4>>^ Do none of the wrongdoers understand?
They devour my people as if they were eating bread.
They do not call on Jehovah.

[[v19-53-5]]

^<<v19-53-5,5>>^ But they will be filled with great terror,
Terror they have never felt before,^&#42;1^
For God will scatter the bones of those attacking you.^&#42;2^
You will put them to shame, for Jehovah has rejected them.

[NOTE]
====
. Or possibly, “Fear where there was nothing to fear.”
. Lit., “camping against you.”
====

[[v19-53-6]]

^<<v19-53-6,6>>^ O that Israel’s salvation may come from Zion!
When Jehovah gathers back his captive people,
Let Jacob be joyful, let Israel rejoice.
