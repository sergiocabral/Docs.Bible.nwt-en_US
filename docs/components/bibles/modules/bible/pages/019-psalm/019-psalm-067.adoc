[[v19-67-0]]
=== Psalm, 67
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

_To the director; to be accompanied with stringed instruments. A melody. A song._

[[v19-67-1]]

^<<v19-67-1,1>>^ God will show us favor and bless us;
He will make his face shine upon us (Selah)

[[v19-67-2]]

^<<v19-67-2,2>>^ So that your way may be known throughout the earth,
Your acts of salvation among all the nations.

[[v19-67-3]]

^<<v19-67-3,3>>^ Let peoples praise you, O God;
Let all the peoples praise you.

[[v19-67-4]]

^<<v19-67-4,4>>^ Let nations rejoice and shout joyfully,
For you will judge the peoples fairly.
You will guide the nations of the earth. (Selah)

[[v19-67-5]]

^<<v19-67-5,5>>^ Let peoples praise you, O God;
Let all the peoples praise you.

[[v19-67-6]]

^<<v19-67-6,6>>^ The earth will give its produce;
God, our God, will bless us.

[[v19-67-7]]

^<<v19-67-7,7>>^ God will bless us,
And all the ends of the earth will fear^&#42;1^ him.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “honor.”
====
