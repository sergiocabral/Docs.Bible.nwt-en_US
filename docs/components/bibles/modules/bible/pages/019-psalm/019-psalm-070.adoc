[[v19-70-0]]
=== Psalm, 70
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

_To the director. Of David, to serve as a reminder.^&#42;1^_

[[v19-70-1]]

^<<v19-70-1,1>>^ O God, save me;
O Jehovah, hurry to help me.

[[v19-70-2]]

^<<v19-70-2,2>>^ May those seeking to take my life^&#42;1^
Be put to shame and disgraced.
May those who delight in my calamity
Retreat in humiliation.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “soul.”
====

[[v19-70-3]]

^<<v19-70-3,3>>^ May those who are saying: “Aha! Aha!”
Be driven back in shame.

[[v19-70-4]]

^<<v19-70-4,4>>^ But let those who are seeking you
Exult and rejoice in you.
May those who love your acts of salvation always say:
“May God be magnified!”

[[v19-70-5]]

^<<v19-70-5,5>>^ But I am helpless and poor;
O God, act quickly in my behalf.
You are my helper and my rescuer;
O Jehovah, do not delay.
