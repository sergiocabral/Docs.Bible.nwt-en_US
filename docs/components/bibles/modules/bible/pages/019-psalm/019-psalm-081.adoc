[[v19-81-0]]
=== Psalm, 81
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

_To the director; upon the Gitʹtith.^&#42;1^ Of Aʹsaph._

[[v19-81-1]]

^<<v19-81-1,1>>^ Shout joyfully to God our strength.
Shout in triumph to the God of Jacob.

[[v19-81-2]]

^<<v19-81-2,2>>^ Begin the music and take a tambourine,
The melodious harp together with the stringed instrument.

[[v19-81-3]]

^<<v19-81-3,3>>^ Blow the horn on the new moon,
On the full moon, for the day of our festival.

[[v19-81-4]]

^<<v19-81-4,4>>^ For it is a decree for Israel,
A ruling of the God of Jacob.

[[v19-81-5]]

^<<v19-81-5,5>>^ He established it as a reminder for Joseph
When He went out against the land of Egypt.
I heard a voice^&#42;1^ that I did not recognize:

[NOTE]
====
. Or “language.”
====

[[v19-81-6]]

^<<v19-81-6,6>>^ “I lifted the burden from his shoulder;
His hands were freed from the basket.

[[v19-81-7]]

^<<v19-81-7,7>>^ In your distress you called, and I rescued you;
I answered you from the thundercloud.^&#42;1^
I tested you at the waters of Merʹi·bah.^&#42;2^ (Selah)

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “in the hidden place of thunder.”
. Meaning “Quarreling.”
====

[[v19-81-8]]

^<<v19-81-8,8>>^ Hear, my people, and I will testify against you.
O Israel, if you would only listen to me.

[[v19-81-9]]

^<<v19-81-9,9>>^ Among you, there will be no strange god,
And you will not bow down to a foreign god.

[[v19-81-10]]

^<<v19-81-10,10>>^ I, Jehovah, am your God,
The one who brought you out of the land of Egypt.
Open your mouth wide, and I will fill it.

[[v19-81-11]]

^<<v19-81-11,11>>^ But my people did not listen to my voice;
Israel would not submit to me.

[[v19-81-12]]

^<<v19-81-12,12>>^ So I let them follow their stubborn hearts;
They did what they thought was right.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “They walked in their counsels.”
====

[[v19-81-13]]

^<<v19-81-13,13>>^ If only my people would listen to me,
If only Israel would walk in my ways!

[[v19-81-14]]

^<<v19-81-14,14>>^ Their enemies I would quickly subdue;
I would turn my hand against their adversaries.

[[v19-81-15]]

^<<v19-81-15,15>>^ Those who hate Jehovah will cringe in his presence,
And their outcome^&#42;1^ will be forever.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “time.”
====

[[v19-81-16]]

^<<v19-81-16,16>>^ But he will feed you^&#42;1^ with the finest^&#42;2^ wheat
And satisfy you with honey from the rock.”

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “him,” that is, God’s people.
. Lit., “the fat of.”
====
