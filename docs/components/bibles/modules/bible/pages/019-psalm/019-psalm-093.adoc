[[v19-93-0]]
=== Psalm, 93
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v19-93-1]]

^<<v19-93-1,1>>^ Jehovah has become King!
With grandeur he is clothed;
Jehovah is clothed with strength;
He wears it like a belt.
The earth^&#42;1^ is firmly established;
It cannot be moved.^&#42;2^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “productive land.”
. Or “cannot totter.”
====

[[v19-93-2]]

^<<v19-93-2,2>>^ Your throne was firmly established long ago;
From eternity you have existed.

[[v19-93-3]]

^<<v19-93-3,3>>^ The rivers have surged, O Jehovah,
The rivers have surged and roared;
The rivers keep surging and pounding.

[[v19-93-4]]

^<<v19-93-4,4>>^ Above the sound of many waters,
Mightier than the breaking waves of the sea,
Jehovah is majestic in the heights.

[[v19-93-5]]

^<<v19-93-5,5>>^ Your reminders are very trustworthy.
Holiness adorns^&#42;1^ your house, O Jehovah, for all times.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “befits.”
====
