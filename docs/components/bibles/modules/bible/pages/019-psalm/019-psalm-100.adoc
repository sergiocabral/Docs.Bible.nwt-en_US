[[v19-100-0]]
=== Psalm, 100
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

_A melody of thanksgiving._

[[v19-100-1]]

^<<v19-100-1,1>>^ Shout in triumph to Jehovah, all the earth.

[[v19-100-2]]

^<<v19-100-2,2>>^ Serve Jehovah with rejoicing.
Come into his presence with joyful shouting.

[[v19-100-3]]

^<<v19-100-3,3>>^ Know^&#42;1^ that Jehovah is God.
He is the one who made us, and we belong to him.^&#42;2^
We are his people and the sheep of his pasture.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “Acknowledge.”
. Or possibly, “and not we ourselves.”
====

[[v19-100-4]]

^<<v19-100-4,4>>^ Come into his gates with thanksgiving,
Into his courtyards with praise.
Give thanks to him; praise his name.

[[v19-100-5]]

^<<v19-100-5,5>>^ For Jehovah is good;
His loyal love endures forever,
And his faithfulness through all generations.
