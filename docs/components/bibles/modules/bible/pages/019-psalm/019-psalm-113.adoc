[[v19-113-0]]
=== Psalm, 113
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v19-113-1]]

^<<v19-113-1,1>>^ Praise Jah!^&#42;1^
Offer praise, you servants of Jehovah,
Praise the name of Jehovah.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “Hallelujah!” “Jah” is a shortened form of the name Jehovah.
====

[[v19-113-2]]

^<<v19-113-2,2>>^ May Jehovah’s name be praised
From now on and forever.

[[v19-113-3]]

^<<v19-113-3,3>>^ From the rising of the sun to its setting,
Let Jehovah’s name be praised.

[[v19-113-4]]

^<<v19-113-4,4>>^ Jehovah is high above all the nations;
His glory is above the heavens.

[[v19-113-5]]

^<<v19-113-5,5>>^ Who is like Jehovah our God,
The one who dwells^&#42;1^ on high?

[NOTE]
====
. Or “sits enthroned.”
====

[[v19-113-6]]

^<<v19-113-6,6>>^ He stoops down to look on heaven and earth,

[[v19-113-7]]

^<<v19-113-7,7>>^ Raising the lowly from the dust.
He lifts up the poor from the ash heap^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or possibly, “garbage dump.”
====

[[v19-113-8]]

^<<v19-113-8,8>>^ In order to make him sit with nobles,
With the nobles of his people.

[[v19-113-9]]

^<<v19-113-9,9>>^ He gives the barren woman a home
As a happy mother with children.^&#42;1^
Praise Jah!^&#42;2^

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “sons.”
. Or “Hallelujah!” “Jah” is a shortened form of the name Jehovah.
====
