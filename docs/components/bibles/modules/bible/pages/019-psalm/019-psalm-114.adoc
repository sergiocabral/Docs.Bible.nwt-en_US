[[v19-114-0]]
=== Psalm, 114
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v19-114-1]]

^<<v19-114-1,1>>^ When Israel went out of Egypt,
The house of Jacob from a people speaking a foreign language,

[[v19-114-2]]

^<<v19-114-2,2>>^ Judah became his sanctuary,^&#42;1^
Israel, his dominion.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “holy place.”
====

[[v19-114-3]]

^<<v19-114-3,3>>^ The sea saw it and fled;
The Jordan turned back.

[[v19-114-4]]

^<<v19-114-4,4>>^ The mountains skipped about like rams,
The hills, like lambs.

[[v19-114-5]]

^<<v19-114-5,5>>^ What made you flee, O sea?
Why, O Jordan, did you turn back?

[[v19-114-6]]

^<<v19-114-6,6>>^ Why did you skip about like rams, O mountains,
Like lambs, O hills?

[[v19-114-7]]

^<<v19-114-7,7>>^ Tremble, O earth, because of the Lord,
Because of the God of Jacob,

[[v19-114-8]]

^<<v19-114-8,8>>^ Who turns the rock into a reedy pool of water,
The flinty rock into springs of water.
