[[v19-120-0]]
=== Psalm, 120
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

_A Song of the Ascents.^&#42;1^_

[[v19-120-1]]

^<<v19-120-1,1>>^ I called to Jehovah in my distress,
And he answered me.

[[v19-120-2]]

^<<v19-120-2,2>>^ O Jehovah, rescue me^&#42;1^ from lying lips
And from the deceitful tongue.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “my soul.”
====

[[v19-120-3]]

^<<v19-120-3,3>>^ What will He do to you, and how will He punish you,^&#42;1^
You deceitful tongue?

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “and what will He add to you?”
====

[[v19-120-4]]

^<<v19-120-4,4>>^ With sharp arrows of a warrior
And burning coals of broomwood.

[[v19-120-5]]

^<<v19-120-5,5>>^ Woe to me, for I have lived as a foreigner in Meʹshech!
I have been dwelling among the tents of Keʹdar.

[[v19-120-6]]

^<<v19-120-6,6>>^ I have^&#42;1^ been dwelling far too long
With those who hate peace.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “My soul has.”
====

[[v19-120-7]]

^<<v19-120-7,7>>^ I am for peace, but when I speak,
They are for war.
