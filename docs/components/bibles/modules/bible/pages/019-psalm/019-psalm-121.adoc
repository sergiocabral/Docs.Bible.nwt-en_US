[[v19-121-0]]
=== Psalm, 121
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

_A Song of the Ascents._

[[v19-121-1]]

^<<v19-121-1,1>>^ I raise my eyes to the mountains.
From where will my help come?

[[v19-121-2]]

^<<v19-121-2,2>>^ My help comes from Jehovah,
The Maker of heaven and earth.

[[v19-121-3]]

^<<v19-121-3,3>>^ He will never allow your foot to slip.^&#42;1^
The One guarding you will never be drowsy.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “totter.”
====

[[v19-121-4]]

^<<v19-121-4,4>>^ Look! He will never be drowsy nor go to sleep,
The One guarding Israel.

[[v19-121-5]]

^<<v19-121-5,5>>^ Jehovah is guarding you.
Jehovah is the shade at your right hand.

[[v19-121-6]]

^<<v19-121-6,6>>^ By day the sun will not strike you,
Nor the moon by night.

[[v19-121-7]]

^<<v19-121-7,7>>^ Jehovah will guard you against all harm.
He will guard your life.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “soul.”
====

[[v19-121-8]]

^<<v19-121-8,8>>^ Jehovah will guard you in all you do^&#42;1^
From now on and forever.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “your going out and your coming in.”
====
