[[v19-123-0]]
=== Psalm, 123
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

_A Song of the Ascents._

[[v19-123-1]]

^<<v19-123-1,1>>^ To you I raise my eyes,
You who are enthroned in the heavens.

[[v19-123-2]]

^<<v19-123-2,2>>^ As the eyes of servants look to the hand of their master,
And the eyes of a servant girl to the hand of her mistress,
So our eyes look to Jehovah our God
Until he shows us favor.

[[v19-123-3]]

^<<v19-123-3,3>>^ Show us favor, O Jehovah, show us favor,
For we have had our full share of contempt.

[[v19-123-4]]

^<<v19-123-4,4>>^ We have had our full share of^&#42;1^ ridicule from the self-assured
And the contempt of the arrogant.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “Our soul has had more than enough.”
====
