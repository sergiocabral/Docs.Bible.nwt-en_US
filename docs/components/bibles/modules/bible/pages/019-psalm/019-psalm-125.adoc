[[v19-125-0]]
=== Psalm, 125
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

_A Song of the Ascents._

[[v19-125-1]]

^<<v19-125-1,1>>^ Those who trust in Jehovah
Are like Mount Zion, which cannot be shaken
But endures forever.

[[v19-125-2]]

^<<v19-125-2,2>>^ Just as mountains surround Jerusalem,
So Jehovah surrounds his people
From now on and forever.

[[v19-125-3]]

^<<v19-125-3,3>>^ The scepter of wickedness will not remain upon the land allotted to the righteous,
So that the righteous do not turn to doing^&#42;1^ what is wrong.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “turn their hands to.”
====

[[v19-125-4]]

^<<v19-125-4,4>>^ Do good, O Jehovah, to those who are good,
To those who are upright in heart.

[[v19-125-5]]

^<<v19-125-5,5>>^ As for those who turn aside to their crooked ways,
Jehovah will remove them along with the wrongdoers.
May there be peace upon Israel.
