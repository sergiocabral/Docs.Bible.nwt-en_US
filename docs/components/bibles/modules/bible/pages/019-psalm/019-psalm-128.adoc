=== Psalm, 128
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

_A Song of the Ascents._

[[v19-128-1]]

^<<v19-128-1,1>>^ Happy is everyone who fears Jehovah,
Who walks in His ways.

[[v19-128-2]]

^<<v19-128-2,2>>^ You will eat what your hands worked hard to produce.
You will be happy and will enjoy prosperity.

[[v19-128-3]]

^<<v19-128-3,3>>^ Your wife will be like a fruitful vine within your house;
Your sons will be like shoots of an olive tree around your table.

[[v19-128-4]]

^<<v19-128-4,4>>^ Look! That is how the man who fears Jehovah
Will be blessed.

[[v19-128-5]]

^<<v19-128-5,5>>^ Jehovah will bless you from Zion.
May you see Jerusalem prosper all the days of your life

[[v19-128-6]]

^<<v19-128-6,6>>^ And see the sons of your sons.
May there be peace upon Israel.
