[[v19-131-0]]
=== Psalm, 131
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

_A Song of the Ascents. Of David._

[[v19-131-1]]

^<<v19-131-1,1>>^ O Jehovah, my heart is not haughty,
Nor are my eyes lofty;
Nor do I aspire to things too great,
Or to things that are beyond me.

[[v19-131-2]]

^<<v19-131-2,2>>^ No, but I have calmed and quieted my soul^&#42;1^
Like a weaned child with its mother;
I am^&#42;2^ contented like a weaned child.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “myself.” See Glossary.
. Or “My soul is.”
====

[[v19-131-3]]

^<<v19-131-3,3>>^ Let Israel wait for Jehovah
From now on and forever.
