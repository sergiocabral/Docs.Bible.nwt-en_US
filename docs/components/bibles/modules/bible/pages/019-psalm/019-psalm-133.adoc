[[v19-133-0]]
=== Psalm, 133
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

_A Song of the Ascents. Of David._

[[v19-133-1]]

^<<v19-133-1,1>>^ Look! How good and how pleasant it is
For brothers to dwell together in unity!

[[v19-133-2]]

^<<v19-133-2,2>>^ It is like fine oil poured on the head
That runs down the beard,
Aaron’s beard,
And runs down onto the collar of his garments.

[[v19-133-3]]

^<<v19-133-3,3>>^ It is like the dew of Herʹmon
That descends on the mountains of Zion.
That is where Jehovah decreed his blessing
—Life everlasting.
