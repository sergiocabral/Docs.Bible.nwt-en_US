[[v19-134-0]]
=== Psalm, 134
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

_A Song of the Ascents._

[[v19-134-1]]

^<<v19-134-1,1>>^ Praise Jehovah,
All you servants of Jehovah,
You who stand in the house of Jehovah during the nights.

[[v19-134-2]]

^<<v19-134-2,2>>^ Raise your hands in holiness^&#42;1^
And praise Jehovah.

[NOTE]
====
. Or possibly, “in the sanctuary.”
====

[[v19-134-3]]

^<<v19-134-3,3>>^ May Jehovah, the Maker of heaven and earth,
Bless you from Zion.
