[[v20-3-0]]
=== Proverbs, 3
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v20-3-1]]

^<<v20-3-1,1>>^ My son, do not forget my teaching,^&#42;1^
And may your heart observe my commandments,

[NOTE]
====
. Or “law.”
====

[[v20-3-2]]

^<<v20-3-2,2>>^ Because they will add many days
And years of life and peace to you.

[[v20-3-3]]

^<<v20-3-3,3>>^ Do not let loyal love and faithfulness^&#42;1^ leave you.
Tie them around your neck;
Write them on the tablet of your heart;

[NOTE]
====
. Or “truth.”
====

[[v20-3-4]]

^<<v20-3-4,4>>^ Then you will find favor and good insight
In the eyes of God and man.

[[v20-3-5]]

^<<v20-3-5,5>>^ Trust in Jehovah with all your heart,
And do not rely^&#42;1^ on your own understanding.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “lean.”
====

[[v20-3-6]]

^<<v20-3-6,6>>^ In all your ways take notice of him,
And he will make your paths straight.

[[v20-3-7]]

^<<v20-3-7,7>>^ Do not become wise in your own eyes.
Fear Jehovah and turn away from bad.

[[v20-3-8]]

^<<v20-3-8,8>>^ It will be a healing to your body^&#42;1^
And refreshment for your bones.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “navel.”
====

[[v20-3-9]]

^<<v20-3-9,9>>^ Honor Jehovah with your valuable things,
With the firstfruits^&#42;1^ of all your produce;^&#42;2^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “very best.”
. Or “income.”
====

[[v20-3-10]]

^<<v20-3-10,10>>^ Then your storehouses will be completely filled,
And your vats^&#42;1^ will overflow with new wine.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “winepresses.”
====

[[v20-3-11]]

^<<v20-3-11,11>>^ My son, do not reject the discipline of Jehovah,
And do not loathe his reproof,

[[v20-3-12]]

^<<v20-3-12,12>>^ For those whom Jehovah loves he reproves,
Just as a father does a son in whom he delights.

[[v20-3-13]]

^<<v20-3-13,13>>^ Happy is the man who finds wisdom
And the man who acquires discernment;

[[v20-3-14]]

^<<v20-3-14,14>>^ To gain it is better than gaining silver,
And having it as profit is better than having gold.

[[v20-3-15]]

^<<v20-3-15,15>>^ It is more precious than corals;^&#42;1^
Nothing you desire can compare to it.

[NOTE]
====
. See Glossary.
====

[[v20-3-16]]

^<<v20-3-16,16>>^ Long life is in its right hand;
Riches and glory are in its left hand.

[[v20-3-17]]

^<<v20-3-17,17>>^ Its ways are pleasant,
And all its paths are peaceful.

[[v20-3-18]]

^<<v20-3-18,18>>^ It is a tree of life to those who take hold of it,
And those who keep firm hold of it will be called happy.

[[v20-3-19]]

^<<v20-3-19,19>>^ Jehovah founded the earth in wisdom.
He solidly established the heavens in discernment.

[[v20-3-20]]

^<<v20-3-20,20>>^ By his knowledge the watery deeps were split apart
And the cloudy skies dripped with dew.

[[v20-3-21]]

^<<v20-3-21,21>>^ My son, do not lose sight of them.^&#42;1^
Safeguard practical wisdom and thinking ability;

[NOTE]
====
. Evidently referring to God’s qualities mentioned in the previous verses.
====

[[v20-3-22]]

^<<v20-3-22,22>>^ They will give you^&#42;1^ life
And be an adornment for your neck;

[NOTE]
====
. Or “your soul.”
====

[[v20-3-23]]

^<<v20-3-23,23>>^ Then you will walk on your way in safety,
And your foot will never stumble.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “strike against anything.”
====

[[v20-3-24]]

^<<v20-3-24,24>>^ When you lie down, you will have no fear;
You will lie down, and your sleep will be pleasant.

[[v20-3-25]]

^<<v20-3-25,25>>^ You will not fear any sudden terror
Nor the storm that is coming on the wicked.

[[v20-3-26]]

^<<v20-3-26,26>>^ For Jehovah will prove to be your source of confidence;
He will keep your foot from being caught.

[[v20-3-27]]

^<<v20-3-27,27>>^ Do not withhold good from those to whom you should give it^&#42;1^
If it is within your power^&#42;2^ to help.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “to whom it is owing.”
. Or “within the power of your hand.”
====

[[v20-3-28]]

^<<v20-3-28,28>>^ Do not say to your neighbor, “Go away; come back later! I will give it to you tomorrow,”
If you can give it now.

[[v20-3-29]]

^<<v20-3-29,29>>^ Do not plot harm against your neighbor
When he lives in a sense of security with you.

[[v20-3-30]]

^<<v20-3-30,30>>^ Do not quarrel with a man for no reason
If he has done nothing bad to you.

[[v20-3-31]]

^<<v20-3-31,31>>^ Do not envy the violent man
Nor choose any of his ways,

[[v20-3-32]]

^<<v20-3-32,32>>^ For Jehovah detests a devious person,
But His close friendship is with the upright.

[[v20-3-33]]

^<<v20-3-33,33>>^ The curse of Jehovah is on the house of the wicked one,
But he blesses the home of the righteous.

[[v20-3-34]]

^<<v20-3-34,34>>^ For he mocks those who ridicule,
But he shows favor to the meek.

[[v20-3-35]]

^<<v20-3-35,35>>^ The wise will inherit honor,
But the stupid ones glorify dishonor.
