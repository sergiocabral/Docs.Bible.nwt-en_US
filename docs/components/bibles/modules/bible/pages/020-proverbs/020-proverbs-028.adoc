[[v20-28-0]]
=== Proverbs, 28
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v20-28-1]]

^<<v20-28-1,1>>^ The wicked flee when no one pursues them,
But the righteous are as confident as a lion.^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “a young lion.”
====

[[v20-28-2]]

^<<v20-28-2,2>>^ When there is transgression^&#42;1^ in the land, it will have one prince after another,
But with the help of a man of discernment and knowledge, a prince^&#42;2^ will long endure.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “revolt.”
. Lit., “he.”
====

[[v20-28-3]]

^<<v20-28-3,3>>^ A poor man who defrauds the lowly
Is like a rain that sweeps away all the food.

[[v20-28-4]]

^<<v20-28-4,4>>^ Those who abandon the law praise the wicked one,
But those who observe the law are indignant with them.

[[v20-28-5]]

^<<v20-28-5,5>>^ Evil men cannot understand justice,
But those who seek Jehovah can understand everything.

[[v20-28-6]]

^<<v20-28-6,6>>^ Better is a poor man who walks in his integrity
Than a rich man whose ways are corrupt.

[[v20-28-7]]

^<<v20-28-7,7>>^ An understanding son observes the law,
But a companion of gluttons disgraces his father.

[[v20-28-8]]

^<<v20-28-8,8>>^ The one who increases his wealth by interest and usury
Amasses it for the one who shows favor to the poor.

[[v20-28-9]]

^<<v20-28-9,9>>^ The one refusing to listen to the law
—Even his prayer is detestable.

[[v20-28-10]]

^<<v20-28-10,10>>^ The one misleading the upright into an evil course will fall into his own pit,
But the blameless will inherit what is good.

[[v20-28-11]]

^<<v20-28-11,11>>^ A rich man is wise in his own eyes,
But a poor man with discernment can see through him.

[[v20-28-12]]

^<<v20-28-12,12>>^ When the righteous triumph, there is great glory,
But when the wicked rise to power, people go into hiding.

[[v20-28-13]]

^<<v20-28-13,13>>^ The one covering over his transgressions will not succeed,
But whoever confesses and abandons them will be shown mercy.

[[v20-28-14]]

^<<v20-28-14,14>>^ Happy is the man who is always on guard,^&#42;1^
But whoever hardens his heart will fall into calamity.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “who is never without fear.”
====

[[v20-28-15]]

^<<v20-28-15,15>>^ Like a growling lion and a charging bear
Is a wicked ruler over a helpless people.

[[v20-28-16]]

^<<v20-28-16,16>>^ A leader without discernment abuses his power,
But the one hating dishonest profit will prolong his life.

[[v20-28-17]]

^<<v20-28-17,17>>^ A man burdened with bloodguilt for taking someone’s life^&#42;1^ will keep fleeing until the grave.^&#42;2^
Let no one support him.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “with the blood of a soul.”
. Or “pit.”
====

[[v20-28-18]]

^<<v20-28-18,18>>^ The one walking faultlessly will be saved,
But the one whose ways are crooked will suddenly fall.

[[v20-28-19]]

^<<v20-28-19,19>>^ The one cultivating his ground will have plenty of bread,
But the one taking up worthless pursuits will have his fill of poverty.

[[v20-28-20]]

^<<v20-28-20,20>>^ A faithful man will receive many blessings,
But the one hastening to get rich will not remain innocent.

[[v20-28-21]]

^<<v20-28-21,21>>^ It is not good to show partiality;
But a man might do wrong for a piece of bread.

[[v20-28-22]]

^<<v20-28-22,22>>^ An envious^&#42;1^ man is eager for wealth,
Not knowing that poverty will overtake him.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “greedy.”
====

[[v20-28-23]]

^<<v20-28-23,23>>^ Whoever reproves a man will afterward find more favor
Than someone who flatters with his tongue.

[[v20-28-24]]

^<<v20-28-24,24>>^ Whoever robs his father and mother and says, “It is not wrong,”
Is a partner of the man who causes ruin.

[[v20-28-25]]

^<<v20-28-25,25>>^ The greedy person^&#42;1^ stirs up dissension,
But whoever relies on Jehovah will prosper.^&#42;2^

[NOTE]
====
. Or possibly, “The arrogant soul.”
. Lit., “will be made fat.”
====

[[v20-28-26]]

^<<v20-28-26,26>>^ Whoever trusts in his own heart is stupid,
But the one who walks in wisdom will escape.

[[v20-28-27]]

^<<v20-28-27,27>>^ Whoever gives to the poor will lack nothing,
But the one who closes his eyes to them will receive many curses.

[[v20-28-28]]

^<<v20-28-28,28>>^ When the wicked rise to power, a man hides himself,
But when they perish, the righteous increase.
