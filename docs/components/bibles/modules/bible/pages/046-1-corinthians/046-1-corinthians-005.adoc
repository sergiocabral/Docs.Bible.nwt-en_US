[[v46-5-0]]
=== The First to the Corinthians, 5
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v46-5-1]]

^<<v46-5-1,1>>^ Actually sexual immorality^&#42;1^ is reported among you, and such immorality^&#42;2^ as is not even found among the nations—of a man living with^&#42;3^ his father’s wife.

[NOTE]
====
. Greek, por·neiʹa. See Glossary.
. Greek, por·neiʹa. See Glossary.
. Lit., “having.”
====

[[v46-5-2]]

^<<v46-5-2,2>>^ And are you proud of it? Should you not rather mourn, so that the man who committed this deed should be taken away from your midst?

[[v46-5-3]]

^<<v46-5-3,3>>^ Although absent in body, I am present in spirit, and I have already judged the man who has done this, as if I were actually with you.

[[v46-5-4]]

^<<v46-5-4,4>>^ When you are gathered together in the name of our Lord Jesus, and knowing that I am with you in spirit along with the power of our Lord Jesus,

[[v46-5-5]]

^<<v46-5-5,5>>^ you must hand such a man over to Satan for the destruction of the flesh, so that the spirit may be saved in the day of the Lord.

[[v46-5-6]]

^<<v46-5-6,6>>^ Your boasting is not good. Do you not know that a little leaven ferments^&#42;1^ the whole batch of dough?

[NOTE]
====
. Or “leavens.”
====

[[v46-5-7]]

^<<v46-5-7,7>>^ Clear away the old leaven so that you may be a new batch, inasmuch as you are free from ferment.^&#42;1^ For, indeed, Christ our Passover lamb has been sacrificed.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “are unleavened.”
====

[[v46-5-8]]

^<<v46-5-8,8>>^ So, then, let us keep the festival, not with old leaven, nor with leaven of badness and wickedness, but with unleavened bread of sincerity and truth.

[[v46-5-9]]

^<<v46-5-9,9>>^ In my letter I wrote you to stop keeping company^&#42;1^ with sexually immoral people,^&#42;2^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “stop associating.”
. See Glossary, “Sexual immorality.”
====

[[v46-5-10]]

^<<v46-5-10,10>>^ not meaning entirely with the sexually immoral people^&#42;1^ of this world or the greedy people or extortioners or idolaters. Otherwise, you would actually have to get out of the world.

[NOTE]
====
. See Glossary, “Sexual immorality.”
====

[[v46-5-11]]

^<<v46-5-11,11>>^ But now I am writing you to stop keeping company^&#42;1^ with anyone called a brother who is sexually immoral^&#42;2^ or a greedy person or an idolater or a reviler^&#42;3^ or a drunkard or an extortioner, not even eating with such a man.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “stop associating.”
. See Glossary, “Sexual immorality.”
. Or “a verbally abusive person.”
====

[[v46-5-12]]

^<<v46-5-12,12>>^ For what do I have to do with judging those outside? Do you not judge those inside,

[[v46-5-13]]

^<<v46-5-13,13>>^ while God judges those outside? “Remove the wicked person from among yourselves.”
