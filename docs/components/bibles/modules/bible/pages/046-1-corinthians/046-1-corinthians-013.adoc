[[v46-13-0]]
=== The First to the Corinthians, 13
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v46-13-1]]

^<<v46-13-1,1>>^ If I speak in the tongues of men and of angels but do not have love, I have become a clanging gong or a clashing cymbal.

[[v46-13-2]]

^<<v46-13-2,2>>^ And if I have the gift of prophecy and understand all the sacred secrets and all knowledge, and if I have all the faith so as to move^&#42;1^ mountains, but do not have love, I am nothing.^&#42;2^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “transplant.”
. Or “useless.”
====

[[v46-13-3]]

^<<v46-13-3,3>>^ And if I give all my belongings to feed others, and if I hand over my body so that I may boast, but do not have love, I do not benefit at all.

[[v46-13-4]]

^<<v46-13-4,4>>^ Love is patient^&#42;1^ and kind. Love is not jealous. It does not brag, does not get puffed up,

[NOTE]
====
. Or “long-suffering.”
====

[[v46-13-5]]

^<<v46-13-5,5>>^ does not behave indecently,^&#42;1^ does not look for its own interests, does not become provoked. It does not keep account of the injury.^&#42;2^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “is not rude.”
. Or “of wrongs.”
====

[[v46-13-6]]

^<<v46-13-6,6>>^ It does not rejoice over unrighteousness, but rejoices with the truth.

[[v46-13-7]]

^<<v46-13-7,7>>^ It bears all things, believes all things, hopes all things, endures all things.

[[v46-13-8]]

^<<v46-13-8,8>>^ Love never fails. But if there are gifts of prophecy, they will be done away with; if there are tongues,^&#42;1^ they will cease; if there is knowledge, it will be done away with.

[NOTE]
====
. That is, miraculous speaking in other languages.
====

[[v46-13-9]]

^<<v46-13-9,9>>^ For we have partial knowledge and we prophesy partially,

[[v46-13-10]]

^<<v46-13-10,10>>^ but when what is complete comes, what is partial will be done away with.

[[v46-13-11]]

^<<v46-13-11,11>>^ When I was a child, I used to speak as a child, to think as a child, to reason as a child; but now that I have become a man, I have done away with the traits of a child.

[[v46-13-12]]

^<<v46-13-12,12>>^ For now we see in hazy outline^&#42;1^ by means of a metal mirror, but then it will be face-to-face. At present I know partially, but then I will know accurately,^&#42;2^ just as I am accurately known.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “indistinctly.”
. Or “fully.”
====

[[v46-13-13]]

^<<v46-13-13,13>>^ Now, however, these three remain: faith, hope, love; but the greatest of these is love.
