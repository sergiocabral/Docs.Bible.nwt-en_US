[[v52-5-0]]
=== The First to the Thessalonians, 5
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v52-5-1]]

^<<v52-5-1,1>>^ Now as for the times and the seasons, brothers, you need nothing to be written to you.

[[v52-5-2]]

^<<v52-5-2,2>>^ For you yourselves know very well that Jehovah’s^&#42;1^ day is coming exactly as a thief in the night.

[NOTE]
====
. See App. A5.
====

[[v52-5-3]]

^<<v52-5-3,3>>^ Whenever it is that they are saying, “Peace and security!” then sudden destruction is to be instantly on them, just like birth pains on a pregnant woman, and they will by no means escape.

[[v52-5-4]]

^<<v52-5-4,4>>^ But you, brothers, you are not in darkness, so that the day should overtake you as it would thieves,

[[v52-5-5]]

^<<v52-5-5,5>>^ for you are all sons of light and sons of day. We belong neither to night nor to darkness.

[[v52-5-6]]

^<<v52-5-6,6>>^ So, then, let us not sleep on as the rest do, but let us stay awake and keep our senses.

[[v52-5-7]]

^<<v52-5-7,7>>^ For those who sleep, sleep at night, and those who get drunk are drunk at night.

[[v52-5-8]]

^<<v52-5-8,8>>^ But as for us who belong to the day, let us keep our senses and put on the breastplate of faith and love and the hope of salvation as a helmet

[[v52-5-9]]

^<<v52-5-9,9>>^ because God assigned us, not to wrath, but to the acquiring of salvation through our Lord Jesus Christ.

[[v52-5-10]]

^<<v52-5-10,10>>^ He died for us, so that whether we stay awake or are asleep,^&#42;1^ we should live together with him.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “asleep in death.”
====

[[v52-5-11]]

^<<v52-5-11,11>>^ Therefore, keep encouraging^&#42;1^ one another and building one another up, just as you are in fact doing.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “comforting.”
====

[[v52-5-12]]

^<<v52-5-12,12>>^ Now we request you, brothers, to show respect for those who are working hard among you and presiding over you in the Lord and admonishing you;

[[v52-5-13]]

^<<v52-5-13,13>>^ and to give them extraordinary consideration in love because of their work. Be peaceable with one another.

[[v52-5-14]]

^<<v52-5-14,14>>^ On the other hand, we urge you, brothers, to warn^&#42;1^ the disorderly, speak consolingly to those who are depressed,^&#42;2^ support the weak, be patient toward all.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “admonish.”
. Or “those who are discouraged.” Lit., “those of little soul.”
====

[[v52-5-15]]

^<<v52-5-15,15>>^ See that no one repays injury for injury to anyone, but always pursue what is good toward one another and to all others.

[[v52-5-16]]

^<<v52-5-16,16>>^ Always be rejoicing.

[[v52-5-17]]

^<<v52-5-17,17>>^ Pray constantly.

[[v52-5-18]]

^<<v52-5-18,18>>^ Give thanks for everything. This is God’s will for you in Christ Jesus.

[[v52-5-19]]

^<<v52-5-19,19>>^ Do not put out the fire of the spirit.

[[v52-5-20]]

^<<v52-5-20,20>>^ Do not treat prophecies with contempt.

[[v52-5-21]]

^<<v52-5-21,21>>^ Make sure of all things; hold fast to what is fine.

[[v52-5-22]]

^<<v52-5-22,22>>^ Abstain from every form of wickedness.

[[v52-5-23]]

^<<v52-5-23,23>>^ May the God of peace himself sanctify you completely. And may the spirit and soul^&#42;1^ and body of you brothers, sound in every respect, be preserved blameless at the presence of our Lord Jesus Christ.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “life.” See Glossary.
====

[[v52-5-24]]

^<<v52-5-24,24>>^ The one who is calling you is faithful, and he will surely do so.

[[v52-5-25]]

^<<v52-5-25,25>>^ Brothers, keep praying for us.

[[v52-5-26]]

^<<v52-5-26,26>>^ Greet all the brothers with a holy kiss.

[[v52-5-27]]

^<<v52-5-27,27>>^ I am putting you under the solemn obligation by the Lord to have this letter read to all the brothers.

[[v52-5-28]]

^<<v52-5-28,28>>^ The undeserved kindness of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you.
