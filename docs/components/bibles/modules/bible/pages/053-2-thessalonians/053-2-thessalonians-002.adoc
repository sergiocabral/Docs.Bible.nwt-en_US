[[v53-2-0]]
=== The Second to the Thessalonians, 2
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v53-2-1]]

^<<v53-2-1,1>>^ However, brothers, concerning the presence of our Lord Jesus Christ and our being gathered together to him, we ask you

[[v53-2-2]]

^<<v53-2-2,2>>^ not to be quickly shaken from your reason nor to be alarmed either by an inspired statement^&#42;1^ or by a spoken message or by a letter appearing to be from us, to the effect that the day of Jehovah^&#42;2^ is here.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “by a spirit.” See Glossary, “Spirit.”
. See App. A5.
====

[[v53-2-3]]

^<<v53-2-3,3>>^ Let no one lead you astray^&#42;1^ in any way, because it will not come unless the apostasy comes first and the man of lawlessness gets revealed, the son of destruction.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “seduce you.”
====

[[v53-2-4]]

^<<v53-2-4,4>>^ He stands in opposition and exalts himself above every so-called god or object of worship,^&#42;1^ so that he sits down in the temple of God, publicly showing himself to be a god.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “reverence.”
====

[[v53-2-5]]

^<<v53-2-5,5>>^ Do you not remember that when I was still with you, I used to tell you these things?

[[v53-2-6]]

^<<v53-2-6,6>>^ And now you know what is acting as a restraint, so that he will be revealed in his own due time.

[[v53-2-7]]

^<<v53-2-7,7>>^ True, the mystery of this lawlessness is already at work, but only until the one who is right now acting as a restraint is out of the way.

[[v53-2-8]]

^<<v53-2-8,8>>^ Then, indeed, the lawless one will be revealed, whom the Lord Jesus will do away with by the spirit of his mouth and bring to nothing by the manifestation of his presence.

[[v53-2-9]]

^<<v53-2-9,9>>^ But the lawless one’s presence is by the operation of Satan with every powerful work and lying signs and wonders^&#42;1^

[NOTE]
====
. Or “portents.”
====

[[v53-2-10]]

^<<v53-2-10,10>>^ and every unrighteous deception for those who are perishing, as a retribution because they did not accept the love of the truth in order that they might be saved.

[[v53-2-11]]

^<<v53-2-11,11>>^ That is why God lets a deceptive influence mislead them so that they may come to believe the lie,

[[v53-2-12]]

^<<v53-2-12,12>>^ in order that they all may be judged because they did not believe the truth but took pleasure in unrighteousness.

[[v53-2-13]]

^<<v53-2-13,13>>^ However, we are obligated always to thank God for you, brothers loved by Jehovah,^&#42;1^ because from the beginning God selected you for salvation by sanctifying you with his spirit and by your faith in the truth.

[NOTE]
====
. See App. A5.
====

[[v53-2-14]]

^<<v53-2-14,14>>^ He called you to this through the good news we declare, so that you may acquire the glory of our Lord Jesus Christ.

[[v53-2-15]]

^<<v53-2-15,15>>^ So, then, brothers, stand firm and maintain your hold on the traditions that you were taught, whether it was by a spoken message or by a letter from us.

[[v53-2-16]]

^<<v53-2-16,16>>^ Moreover, may our Lord Jesus Christ himself and God our Father, who loved us and gave everlasting comfort and good hope by means of undeserved kindness,

[[v53-2-17]]

^<<v53-2-17,17>>^ comfort your hearts and make you firm^&#42;1^ in every good deed and word.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “strengthen you.”
====
