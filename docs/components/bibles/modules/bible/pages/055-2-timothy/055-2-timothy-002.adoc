[[v55-2-0]]
=== The Second to Timothy, 2
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v55-2-1]]

^<<v55-2-1,1>>^ You, therefore, my child, keep on acquiring power in the undeserved kindness that is in Christ Jesus;

[[v55-2-2]]

^<<v55-2-2,2>>^ and the things you heard from me that were supported by many witnesses, these things entrust to faithful men, who, in turn, will be adequately qualified to teach others.

[[v55-2-3]]

^<<v55-2-3,3>>^ As a fine soldier of Christ Jesus, take your part in suffering adversity.

[[v55-2-4]]

^<<v55-2-4,4>>^ No man serving as a soldier involves himself^&#42;1^ in the commercial businesses^&#42;2^ of life, in order to gain the approval of the one who enrolled him as a soldier.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “gets entangled.”
. Or possibly, “the everyday activities.”
====

[[v55-2-5]]

^<<v55-2-5,5>>^ And even in the games, anyone who competes is not crowned unless he has competed according to the rules.

[[v55-2-6]]

^<<v55-2-6,6>>^ The hardworking farmer must be the first to partake of the fruits.

[[v55-2-7]]

^<<v55-2-7,7>>^ Give constant thought to what I am saying; the Lord will give you understanding^&#42;1^ in all things.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “discernment.”
====

[[v55-2-8]]

^<<v55-2-8,8>>^ Remember that Jesus Christ was raised up from the dead and was David’s offspring,^&#42;1^ according to the good news I preach,

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “of David’s seed.”
====

[[v55-2-9]]

^<<v55-2-9,9>>^ for which I am suffering and being imprisoned as a criminal. Nevertheless, the word of God is not bound.

[[v55-2-10]]

^<<v55-2-10,10>>^ For this reason I go on enduring all things for the sake of the chosen ones, so that they too may obtain the salvation that is through Christ Jesus, along with everlasting glory.

[[v55-2-11]]

^<<v55-2-11,11>>^ This saying is trustworthy: Certainly if we died together, we will also live together;

[[v55-2-12]]

^<<v55-2-12,12>>^ if we go on enduring, we will also rule together as kings; if we deny, he will also deny us;

[[v55-2-13]]

^<<v55-2-13,13>>^ if we are unfaithful, he remains faithful, for he cannot deny himself.

[[v55-2-14]]

^<<v55-2-14,14>>^ Keep reminding them of these things, instructing^&#42;1^ them before God not to fight about words, something of no usefulness at all because it harms^&#42;2^ those listening.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “bearing thorough witness to.”
. Or “destroys; overturns.”
====

[[v55-2-15]]

^<<v55-2-15,15>>^ Do your utmost to present yourself approved to God, a workman with nothing to be ashamed of, handling the word of the truth aright.

[[v55-2-16]]

^<<v55-2-16,16>>^ But reject empty speeches that violate what is holy, for they will lead to more and more ungodliness,

[[v55-2-17]]

^<<v55-2-17,17>>^ and their word will spread like gangrene. Hy·me·naeʹus and Phi·leʹtus are among them.

[[v55-2-18]]

^<<v55-2-18,18>>^ These men have deviated from the truth, saying that the resurrection has already occurred, and they are subverting the faith of some.

[[v55-2-19]]

^<<v55-2-19,19>>^ Despite that, the solid foundation of God remains standing, having this seal, “Jehovah^&#42;1^ knows those who belong to him,” and, “Let everyone calling on the name of Jehovah^&#42;2^ renounce unrighteousness.”

[NOTE]
====
. See App. A5.
. See App. A5.
====

[[v55-2-20]]

^<<v55-2-20,20>>^ Now in a large house there are utensils^&#42;1^ not only of gold and silver but also of wood and earthenware, and some for an honorable use but others for a use lacking honor.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “vessels.”
====

[[v55-2-21]]

^<<v55-2-21,21>>^ So if anyone keeps clear of the latter ones, he will be an instrument^&#42;1^ for an honorable use, sanctified, useful to his owner, prepared for every good work.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “a utensil; a vessel.”
====

[[v55-2-22]]

^<<v55-2-22,22>>^ So flee from youthful desires, but pursue righteousness, faith, love, peace, along with those who call on the Lord out of a clean heart.

[[v55-2-23]]

^<<v55-2-23,23>>^ Further, reject foolish and ignorant debates, knowing that they produce fights.

[[v55-2-24]]

^<<v55-2-24,24>>^ For a slave of the Lord does not need to fight, but needs to be gentle^&#42;1^ toward all, qualified to teach, showing restraint when wronged,

[NOTE]
====
. Or “tactful.”
====

[[v55-2-25]]

^<<v55-2-25,25>>^ instructing with mildness those not favorably disposed. Perhaps God may give them repentance^&#42;1^ leading to an accurate knowledge of truth,

[NOTE]
====
. Or “a change of mind.”
====

[[v55-2-26]]

^<<v55-2-26,26>>^ and they may come to their senses and escape from the snare of the Devil, seeing that they have been caught alive by him to do his will.
