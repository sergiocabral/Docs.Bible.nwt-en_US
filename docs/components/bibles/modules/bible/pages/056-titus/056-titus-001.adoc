[[v56-1-0]]
=== To Titus, 1
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v56-1-1]]

^<<v56-1-1,1>>^ Paul, a slave of God and an apostle of Jesus Christ according to the faith of God’s chosen ones and the accurate knowledge of the truth that is according to godly devotion

[[v56-1-2]]

^<<v56-1-2,2>>^ and is based on a hope of the everlasting life that God, who cannot lie, promised long ago;

[[v56-1-3]]

^<<v56-1-3,3>>^ but in his own due time, he made his word known through the preaching entrusted to me according to the command of our Savior, God;

[[v56-1-4]]

^<<v56-1-4,4>>^ to Titus, a genuine child according to the faith we share:
May you have undeserved kindness and peace from God the Father and Christ Jesus our Savior.

[[v56-1-5]]

^<<v56-1-5,5>>^ I left you in Crete so that you would correct the things that were defective^&#42;1^ and make appointments of elders in city after city, as I instructed you:

[NOTE]
====
. Or “deficient.”
====

[[v56-1-6]]

^<<v56-1-6,6>>^ if there is any man free from accusation, a husband of one wife, having believing children who are not accused of debauchery^&#42;1^ or rebelliousness.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “wildness.”
====

[[v56-1-7]]

^<<v56-1-7,7>>^ For as God’s steward, an overseer must be free from accusation, not self-willed, not quick-tempered, not a drunkard, not violent,^&#42;1^ not greedy of dishonest gain,

[NOTE]
====
. Or “a smiter.”
====

[[v56-1-8]]

^<<v56-1-8,8>>^ but hospitable, a lover of goodness, sound in mind,^&#42;1^ righteous, loyal, self-controlled,

[NOTE]
====
. Or “having good judgment; sensible.”
====

[[v56-1-9]]

^<<v56-1-9,9>>^ holding firmly to the faithful word^&#42;1^ as respects his art of teaching, so that he may be able both to encourage^&#42;2^ by the teaching that is wholesome^&#42;3^ and to reprove those who contradict.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “the trustworthy message.”
. Or “exhort.”
. Or “healthful; beneficial.”
====

[[v56-1-10]]

^<<v56-1-10,10>>^ For there are many rebellious men, profitless talkers, and deceivers, especially those who adhere to the circumcision.

[[v56-1-11]]

^<<v56-1-11,11>>^ It is necessary to shut their mouths, because these very men keep on subverting entire households by teaching things they should not for the sake of dishonest gain.

[[v56-1-12]]

^<<v56-1-12,12>>^ A certain one of them, their own prophet, said: “Creʹtans are always liars, injurious wild beasts, idle gluttons.”

[[v56-1-13]]

^<<v56-1-13,13>>^ This witness is true. For this very reason, keep on reproving them with severity so that they may be healthy in the faith,

[[v56-1-14]]

^<<v56-1-14,14>>^ paying no attention to Jewish fables and commandments of men who turn away from the truth.

[[v56-1-15]]

^<<v56-1-15,15>>^ All things are clean to clean people; but to those who are defiled and faithless, nothing is clean, for both their minds and their consciences are defiled.

[[v56-1-16]]

^<<v56-1-16,16>>^ They publicly declare that they know God, but they disown him by their works, because they are detestable and disobedient and not approved for good work of any sort.
