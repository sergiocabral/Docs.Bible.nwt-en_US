[[v58-13-0]]
=== To the Hebrews, 13
// nwt 2013, nwt - New World Translation of the Holy Scriptures

[[v58-13-1]]

^<<v58-13-1,1>>^ Let your brotherly love continue.

[[v58-13-2]]

^<<v58-13-2,2>>^ Do not forget hospitality,^&#42;1^ for through it some unknowingly entertained angels.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “kindness to strangers.”
====

[[v58-13-3]]

^<<v58-13-3,3>>^ Keep in mind those in prison,^&#42;1^ as though you were imprisoned with them, and those being mistreated, since you yourselves also are in the body.^&#42;2^

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “the bound ones; those in bonds.”
. Or possibly, “as if you were suffering with them.”
====

[[v58-13-4]]

^<<v58-13-4,4>>^ Let marriage be honorable among all, and let the marriage bed be without defilement, for God will judge sexually immoral people^&#42;1^ and adulterers.

[NOTE]
====
. See Glossary, “Sexual immorality.”
====

[[v58-13-5]]

^<<v58-13-5,5>>^ Let your way of life be free of the love of money, while you are content with the present things. For he has said: “I will never leave you, and I will never abandon you.”

[[v58-13-6]]

^<<v58-13-6,6>>^ So that we may be of good courage and say: “Jehovah^&#42;1^ is my helper; I will not be afraid. What can man do to me?”

[NOTE]
====
. See App. A5.
====

[[v58-13-7]]

^<<v58-13-7,7>>^ Remember those who are taking the lead among you, who have spoken the word of God to you, and as you contemplate how their conduct turns out, imitate their faith.

[[v58-13-8]]

^<<v58-13-8,8>>^ Jesus Christ is the same yesterday and today, and forever.

[[v58-13-9]]

^<<v58-13-9,9>>^ Do not be led astray by various and strange teachings, for it is better for the heart to be strengthened by undeserved kindness than by foods,^&#42;1^ which do not benefit those occupied with them.

[NOTE]
====
. That is, rules about food.
====

[[v58-13-10]]

^<<v58-13-10,10>>^ We have an altar from which those who offer sacred service at the tent have no authority to eat.

[[v58-13-11]]

^<<v58-13-11,11>>^ For the bodies of those animals whose blood is taken into the holy place as a sin offering by the high priest are burned up outside the camp.

[[v58-13-12]]

^<<v58-13-12,12>>^ Therefore, Jesus also suffered outside the city gate in order to sanctify the people with his own blood.

[[v58-13-13]]

^<<v58-13-13,13>>^ Let us, then, go to him outside the camp, bearing the reproach he bore,

[[v58-13-14]]

^<<v58-13-14,14>>^ for we do not have here a city that remains, but we are earnestly seeking the one to come.

[[v58-13-15]]

^<<v58-13-15,15>>^ Through him let us always offer to God a sacrifice of praise, that is, the fruit of our lips that make public declaration to his name.

[[v58-13-16]]

^<<v58-13-16,16>>^ Moreover, do not forget to do good and to share what you have with others, for God is well-pleased with such sacrifices.

[[v58-13-17]]

^<<v58-13-17,17>>^ Be obedient to those who are taking the lead among you and be submissive, for they are keeping watch over you^&#42;1^ as those who will render an account, so that they may do this with joy and not with sighing, for this would be damaging to you.

[NOTE]
====
. Or “your souls.”
====

[[v58-13-18]]

^<<v58-13-18,18>>^ Keep praying for us, for we trust we have an honest^&#42;1^ conscience, as we wish to conduct ourselves honestly in all things.

[NOTE]
====
. Lit., “good.”
====

[[v58-13-19]]

^<<v58-13-19,19>>^ But I especially urge you to pray so that I may be restored to you the sooner.

[[v58-13-20]]

^<<v58-13-20,20>>^ Now may the God of peace, who brought up from the dead the great shepherd of the sheep, our Lord Jesus, with the blood of an everlasting covenant,

[[v58-13-21]]

^<<v58-13-21,21>>^ equip you with every good thing to do his will, working in us through Jesus Christ what is well-pleasing in his sight, to whom be the glory forever and ever. Amen.

[[v58-13-22]]

^<<v58-13-22,22>>^ Now I urge you, brothers, to listen patiently to this word of encouragement, for I have written you a short letter.

[[v58-13-23]]

^<<v58-13-23,23>>^ I want you to know that our brother Timothy has been released. If he comes soon, I will be with him when I see you.

[[v58-13-24]]

^<<v58-13-24,24>>^ Give my greetings to all those who are taking the lead among you and to all the holy ones. Those in Italy send you their greetings.

[[v58-13-25]]

^<<v58-13-25,25>>^ The undeserved kindness be with all of you.
